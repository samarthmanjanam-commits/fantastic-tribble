The United Arab Emirates is making significant strides in eco-conscious architecture, with each emirate implementing unique sustainability initiatives.

  • All new buildings to meet Estidama Pearl standards by 2030
  • Net zero carbon government buildings by 2030

    About UAE's Sustainable Architecture

    The UAE is transitioning from iconic megastructures to sustainable architecture that respects the local environment and culture. Each emirate has developed unique approaches to green building that combine modern technology with traditional design principles.
